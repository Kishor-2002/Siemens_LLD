
You need to implement the following:
1. Order Creation
• An order contains:
○ OrderId
○ CustomerName
○ List of Items (Name, Price, Quantity)
 
2. Total Calculation
• Calculate total price of the order
 
3. Discount System
Apply discounts based on customer type:
• Regular Customer → no discount
• Silver Customer → 10% discount
• Gold Customer → 20% discount
Customer type should be extensible (new types may come later)
 
4. Tax Calculation
• Flat 5% tax applied on final discounted amount
 
5. Output
Return final payable amount
